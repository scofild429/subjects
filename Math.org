Laplace transfromation : $f(t) \rightarrow L(w) = \int_{-\infty}^{\infty}f(t)e^{-\sigma t}e^{-j\omega t} dt$

or $f(t)\rightarrow L(w) =\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}f(w)e^{-st}dt$, where $s=\sigma + j \omega$
